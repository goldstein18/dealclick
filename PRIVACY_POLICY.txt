1. INTRODUCTION

DealClick ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

Personal Information:
- Name and contact information (email, phone number, WhatsApp)
- Profile photo/avatar
- Company affiliation
- Professional specialties
- Geographic location (state/region)
- Agent type and credentials

Property Information:
- Property listings and images
- Property requirements
- Transaction details

Technical Information:
- Device information
- IP address
- Usage statistics
- App performance data

3. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

We use your information to:
- Provide and maintain our service
- Create and manage your account
- Connect you with other real estate professionals
- Display property listings and requirements
- Send notifications about relevant opportunities
- Improve our application
- Comply with legal obligations

4. DATA STORAGE

Your data is stored securely using:
- Supabase (PostgreSQL database)
- Backblaze B2 (image storage)
- Industry-standard encryption

5. DATA SHARING

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information with:
- Other verified agents on the platform (public profile information only)
- Service providers who assist in operating our application
- Legal authorities when required by law

6. YOUR RIGHTS

You have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Correct inaccurate data
- Delete your account and data
- Opt-out of marketing communications
- Export your data

7. SECURITY

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information, including:
- Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/SSL)
- Secure authentication (JWT tokens)
- Regular security audits
- Access controls and monitoring

8. BIOMETRIC DATA

If you enable Face ID/Touch ID for login:
- Biometric data is stored locally on your device only
- We do not access or store biometric information on our servers
- You can disable biometric login at any time

9. CAMERA AND PHOTO LIBRARY

We request access to:
- Camera: To take photos of properties and update your profile picture
- Photo Library: To select images for property listings and profile

Images are uploaded to our secure storage and are not shared with third parties except as displayed in property listings.

10. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

Our service is intended for professional real estate agents (18+). We do not knowingly collect information from children under 18.

11. INTERNATIONAL TRANSFERS

Your data may be transferred and processed in countries outside your country of residence, including the United States and United Kingdom. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place.

12. UPDATES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app and updating the "Last Updated" date.
